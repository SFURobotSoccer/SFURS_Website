SFU Robot Soccer

SFU Robot Soccer is an organization that consists of mainly engineering and computing science students, but also includes business, communications, and other majors.

Our community is built to foster innovation and to compete in the international RoboCup competitions.
We are based at the SFU Burnaby campus, and welcome any dedicated students to join us.
Through hard work, strong leadership, and efficient teamwork we will achieve our victory on the world stage.
